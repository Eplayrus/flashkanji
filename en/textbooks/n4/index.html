Apps and Interfaces

Amazon · shopping, cart, checkout Rakuten · items and discounts Instagram · photos and posts

Hiragana and Katakana

Hiragana

  • もの · mono — thing/item
  • ところ · tokoro — place
  • よてい · yotei — plan

Katakana

  • セール · seeru — sale
  • ショップ · shoppu — shop
  • フォト · foto — photo

Anchor Words

  • 買い物 · kaimono — shopping. cart and order history
  • 写真 · shashin — photo. galleries and profiles
  • 場所 · basho — place. maps and meetings

Blank Sentence Drills

  • アプリで___をします。
    アプリで かいものを します。
    I shop in the app.
  • プロフィールに___を入れます。
    プロフィールに しゃしんを いれます。
    I add a photo to the profile.

Textbook Goals

  • Read common verb stems and compounds with 物, 者, 場, 所.
  • Handle sentences where the blank appears in the middle, not only at the start.
  • Recognize words from marketplaces, maps, and messengers.

Level Focus

At N4, the meaning of the compound matters more than a single character. 買物, 場所, 住所, 予定, 写真, and 連絡 appear often in real interfaces.

  • After answering, open details and check the word examples.
  • Note one or two words where the reading behaves unusually.

SRS Rhythm

N4 brings more errors because words look similar. Forgot is not a punishment; it brings the card back sooner and strengthens memory.

  • If you confuse the reading, choose Forgot.
  • If the meaning is clear but the reading is not, review sooner.
