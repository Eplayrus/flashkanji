Textbook Goals

  • Recognize rare forms and similar components consistently.
  • Infer word meaning from context even when the reading is not perfect.
  • Keep reviews manageable through honest two-button SRS.

What Changes At N1

N1 is less a list of characters and more real reading. Nuance matters: 称, 納, 樹, 論, 認, 議, 影, and related families.

  • Compare close words and do not fear sending a card back to review.
  • Use sentence practice and custom sources as your main practice mode.

Retention Strategy

At N1, fewer new cards and more context often works better. If the same type repeats too often, switch modes: writing, sentences, dictionary.

  • Favorites and writing help with visually similar characters.
  • The system stretches intervals automatically when success becomes high.
