Hiragana and Katakana

Hiragana

  • とうろく · touroku — registration
  • せいげん · seigen — restriction
  • しょうにん · shounin — approval

Katakana

  • アカウント · akaunto — account
  • パスワード · pasuwaado — password
  • アクセス · akusesu — access

Anchor Words

  • 登録 · touroku — registration. account creation
  • 制限 · seigen — restriction. access rights
  • 承認 · shounin — approval. moderation and requests

Blank Sentence Drills

  • アカウントを___します。
    アカウントを とうろくします。
    I register an account.
  • アクセスが___されています。
    アクセスが せいげんされています。
    Access is restricted.

Textbook Goals

  • Parse formal words from notices and rules.
  • Connect kanji with situations: registration, confirmation, restriction, permission.
  • Speed up recognition of rarer forms through SRS and writing.

Interface Words

N2 includes many words like 登録, 承認, 制限, 削除, 変更, 通知, 管理. They are essential in real apps, account dashboards, and help pages.

  • Learn the function of the word: button, warning, status, or action.
  • Read examples as whole phrases, not isolated kanji.

Memory Depth

For N2, honest answers matter. Remember should mean you know the meaning and can recall at least one reading.

  • If you recognize the shape but cannot read it, review sooner.
  • Use favorites for difficult characters.
