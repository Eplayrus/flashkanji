Hiragana and Katakana

Hiragana

  • ひつよう · hitsuyou — necessary
  • かくにん · kakunin — confirmation
  • へんこう · henkou — change

Katakana

  • チェック · chekku — check
  • エラー · eraa — error
  • ルール · ruuru — rule

Anchor Words

  • 確認 · kakunin — confirmation. login, forms, moderation
  • 変更 · henkou — change. settings and schedules
  • 必要 · hitsuyou — necessity. form errors and requirements

Blank Sentence Drills

  • メールで___してください。
    メールで かくにんしてください。
    Please confirm by email.
  • 予定を___しました。
    よていを へんこうしました。
    I changed the plan.

Textbook Goals

  • Read 2-4 kanji words as one unit.
  • Understand messages about conditions, reasons, changes, and results.
  • Use sentence practice for real context.

Context Beats Isolated Characters

N3 often tests whether you understand the situation. 確認, 変更, 必要, 可能, 禁止, and 連絡 appear in forms, warnings, and settings.

  • After a card, check where the character appears in apps.
  • Add your own sentences from websites and interfaces.

How To Review

If a word feels familiar but uncertain, choose Forgot. The system will schedule a short delay.

  • Compare pairs: 必 / 心, 場 / 湯, 特 / 待.
  • Writing practice helps lock in complex shapes.
